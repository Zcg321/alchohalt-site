What’s inside

Quiet tools that stay out of your way

Every feature on this page is in the app you’ll download — no mock-ups, no teasers.

Calm, private tracking

Log a drink in two taps. Your streak, the money you’ve kept, and your own patterns — gently, and without a scoreboard. Your numbers are yours to see. Individual results vary.

Outlasted

A craving is a wave — it crests and it passes. When one hits, open Outlasted and ride it out while a quiet timer keeps you company. Every wave you outlast counts, and that count never goes back down.

Journal, mood & HALT

Write a line about the day. Note how you feel and what set it off — Hungry, Angry, Lonely, Tired. Over time, your own rhythms come into view.

My Person

Choose one person you trust and keep a few messages ready for a hard day. You’re always the one who decides to send — nothing is ever sent for you.

Real help, never locked

988 in the US, plus SAMHSA, AA, SMART Recovery, and lines for the UK, Ireland, Australia, and Canada — one tap from home, never behind a paywall or a login.

Private by default

No ads. No trackers. No account. A panel inside the app lists every feature that could ever use the network, and shows you it’s off.

How it works

Three quiet steps

1

Log it in two taps

Open the app, tap what you had. No forms, no lecture — then get on with your day.

2

Notice your patterns

Add a mood or a HALT tag if you like. The app finds the patterns on your device — that analysis never leaves your phone.

3

Get through the hard moments

When a craving hits, ride it out with Outlasted. And real crisis lines are always one tap away.

Your data stays yours

Privacy isn’t a setting. It’s the whole design.

Alchohalt has no server that could receive your entries. What you log stays on your phone unless you decide to export it — and even then, the copy you make is the only one that ever leaves your device.

Read the full privacy policy →

  • On your device by default.No account, no sign-up, no profile — there’s no server to hold your data.
  • No ads, no trackers, no analytics.None are built in — and a test in the code keeps it that way.
  • Nothing sold, nothing shared.There are no advertising partners, and your data is never sold or rented.
  • No social feed, no leaderboard.Your progress is yours alone — there’s no one to perform for.
  • See it for yourself.A privacy panel lists every feature that could touch the network and shows it switched off.
  • Your history is portable.Export everything as JSON or CSV whenever you want. You own your data — keeping it shouldn’t cost extra.

If you need more than an app

Alchohalt is a self-tracking tool, not medical care, and it’s not a substitute for professional treatment. Alcohol withdrawal can be medically dangerous — if you’re a heavy drinker thinking about stopping or cutting back sharply, please talk to a doctor first.

If you or someone near you is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number. The app keeps real crisis and support lines one tap away, always.
